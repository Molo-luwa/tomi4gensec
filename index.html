Dear fellow students,
I, Adeyemi Oluwatomi, humbly present myself as a candidate for the position of General Secretary. My vision is to strengthen the administrative structure of the department through organized documentation, effective communication, and responsible coordination.

  • Accurate documentation of meetings, proper record keeping, timely official correspondence in line with the constitution.
  • Organized records of departmental activities and past minutes to serve as reference for future administrations – ensure continuity.
  • Work closely with executives to ensure smooth coordination of events through documentation & communication.
  • Compile a simple documentation guide to help present/incoming executives understand administrative procedures.

Serving previously as a Senator and Senate Scribe has given me practical experience in documentation, organization, and working within structured systems. These experiences have prepared me to effectively handle the responsibilities of the office of General Secretary.

This position is not just about keeping records, but about ensuring the administrative system remains ORGANIZED, RELIABLE, and EFFICIENT.
I therefore humbly seek your support and vote.
Thank you.
